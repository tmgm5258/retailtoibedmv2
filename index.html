Entry Criteria
📥
Retail Client Enters Journey
Automated trigger — filter-based, real-time entry
✅ Confirmed with Sichen & Nick
👥 Audience
All retail clients who match the filter conditions below. Phase 1.0 — pure mass blast, no behavioural segmentation. Segmentation (by trading activity, FTD, account age, etc.) deferred to Phase 2.0.
🔄 Entry Method
Automated trigger. SFMC continuously monitors the MasterDataExtension — the moment a client's data matches all filter conditions, they automatically enter the journey at their own personal Day 0. No manual batch pulls. No waiting for a monthly send. Each client gets their own timeline.
📊 Data Source
MasterDataExtension (SFMC, migrating to HubSpot) — owned by Nick Gao. Journey & filter setup owned by Sichen Zhang.
🎯 Filter Conditions
Client must match ALL of the following conditions:
ID_Verified = Verified Only target clients who have completed KYC verification (hygiene — no unfinished onboarding)
Is_IB = false Exclude clients who are already IBs — they've already converted, no point targeting them again
Member_Lead_Source ≠ contains "IB" Exclude clients who came in through an IB referral channel — avoid overlap with the existing IB ecosystem
MailingCountry IN (target markets) Only target clients in our Phase 1.0 regional scope (see target markets below)
📝 Note: The current live filter has Registration_Time > 1/1/2024. For Phase 1.0 we propose removing this cutoff to include the full back catalogue of pre-2024 retail clients — maximising audience for clean baseline data.
🌏 Target Markets
Retail clients across SEA, MENA, and EN markets — matching our 6-language content plan (EN, ID, KO, VN, TH, AR):
🌏 SEA
Thailand · Vietnam · Indonesia · Malaysia · Singapore · Philippines · South Korea
🌐 EN Markets
Additional English-speaking markets where TMGM operates (excl. UK & ASIC regions)
🕌 MENA
Saudi Arabia · UAE · Egypt · Kuwait · Qatar · Bahrain · Oman · Jordan · Lebanon
🚫 Hygiene Exclusions
Standard list hygiene — applied automatically by SFMC before send:
Existing IBs Mainland China Hong Kong UK Unsubscribed users Closed accounts Hard-bounced emails
Note: These are hygiene-only — no behavioural segmentation (account age, FTD, trading volume) for Phase 1.0.
