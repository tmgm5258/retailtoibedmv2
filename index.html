🧠 Why this context matters for our Retail-to-IB journey
By the time our E1 lands, a client has already received 21–23 emails from TMGM across 4 onboarding journeys. They've been told about trading tools, security, rewards, deposit bonuses, and leverage.

Our E1 subject "What would an extra $500/month look like?" must cut through this fatigue by doing something none of the upstream emails do: position earnings from referrals, not from trading more.

This is also why our 7-day buffer after FTD matters — it gives the client space from the KYC-FTD journey's aggressive deposit/bonus push before we arrive with a different value proposition.

🔄 Entry Method
Automated trigger. SFMC continuously monitors the MasterDataExtension — the moment a client's data matches all filter conditions, they automatically enter the journey at their own personal Day 0. No manual batch pulls. No waiting for a monthly send. Each client gets their own timeline.

🎯 Filter Conditions
Client must match ALL of the following conditions:
